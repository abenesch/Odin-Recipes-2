Snickerdoodle!

Ingredients

  • Cream of tartar
  • Baking soda
  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon sugar mixture (for coating)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

  2. Combine Dry Ingredients:

    In a bowl, whisk together flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and a pinch of salt.

  3. Cream Butter and Sugar:

    In a large mixing bowl, cream together softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add Eggs and Vanilla:

    Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract and mix until well combined.

  5. Incorporate Dry Ingredients:

    Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

  6. Chill the Dough:

    Chill the cookie dough in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes.

  7. Shape into Balls:

    Shape the dough into balls, and roll each ball in a cinnamon sugar mixture to coat evenly.

  8. Place on Baking Sheets:

    Place the coated dough balls on the prepared baking sheets, leaving enough space between each cookie.

  9. Bake:

    Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are set but the centers are still soft.

  10. Cool and Enjoy:

    Allow the snickerdoodles to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy your delicious snickerdoodles!
